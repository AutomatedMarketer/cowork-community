# Next move block template (lead-scan)

Every `/lead-scan` run ends with this block. Specific. Actionable. Time-bound. Different from `/community-check`'s next-move because this skill never engages — the next move is always something the user does manually.

```markdown
---

## ⚡ Next move

**Subject:** {{WHAT — e.g. "The top-scored lead (Sarah K., score 5)"}}
**Verb:** {{ACTION — e.g. "DM her using the suggested opener — edit it first if it doesn't feel right"}}
**Timing:** {{WHEN — e.g. "Within the next hour while the post is fresh"}}
**Why:** {{REASON — e.g. "Reddit posts decay fast — older than 6 hours, your DM is more likely to be ignored"}}

---
```

## Pick the right shape by outcome

### High-quality day (1+ score-5 lead)
- Subject: The top lead (`<name>`, score 5)
- Verb: DM with the suggested opener (edit if needed) and reply here when done
- Timing: Next hour
- Why: Post is fresh — DM open rates drop sharply after 6 hours

### Mid day (no 5s, 1+ score-4)
- Subject: The top score-4 lead
- Verb: Public comment first to build context, then DM tomorrow if they respond
- Timing: Today + tomorrow follow-up
- Why: No budget signal = warmer relationship needed before pitching anything

### Low day (only score-3s)
- Subject: This week's leads pipeline
- Verb: Read tomorrow's leads file with today's open in tabs — compound the watching
- Timing: Tomorrow morning at scan time
- Why: Score-3 leads sometimes evolve into score-4/5 as comments accumulate

### Zero leads day
- Subject: The ICP pain phrases
- Verb: Edit `business-brain.md` to add 2-3 more variants of how your ICP describes their pain
- Timing: Tonight before bed
- Why: Empty days usually mean the pattern matcher is too narrow — richer phrases catch more

### Flagged-only day (only ⚠️ leads found)
- Subject: The flagged leads
- Verb: Read them carefully — these are NOT cold-DM situations, but some may warrant a check-in if you already know the person
- Timing: When you have 10 quiet minutes
- Why: Flagged leads exist for a reason; review-with-judgment matters more than scan speed
```

## Rendering rules

- Always show one specific next move, not a list of options.
- Never end with "let me know if you need anything."
- Time-bound. "Within an hour" / "Tonight" / "Tomorrow morning" — never "soon."
